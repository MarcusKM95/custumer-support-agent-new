Refund policy:
Customers can request a refund within 30 days of purchase if they have a valid receipt. Refunds are not available after 30 days unless required by law.

Shipping policy:
Standard shipping takes 3 to 5 business days. Express shipping takes 1 to 2 business days.

Return policy:
Products must be returned in original condition with all accessories included. Used or damaged products may be rejected.

Complaint policy:
If a customer is angry or dissatisfied, the support agent should remain polite, apologize for the inconvenience, and offer to escalate the case to a human support specialist.

Warranty policy:
Electronic products include a 2-year warranty for manufacturing defects. The warranty does not cover accidental damage or misuse.
